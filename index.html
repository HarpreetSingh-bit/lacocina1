About Us

La Cocina & Cantina, a Mexican restaurant situated at 432 Broadway E, Seattle, WA 98102, has been a beloved establishment since 1980. We take pride in serving our customers with a wide array of delectable Mexican dishes, refreshing drinks, convenient reservation options, and hassle-free online ordering services. Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a favorite dining spot in the heart of Seattle.
